# Human Oversight Policy

Humans retain accountability for approval, risk acceptance, review decisions, and repository hardening. Agents can assist, but they do not replace accountable roles.

## Oversight requirements

- every non-trivial task has named human owners
- every `medium`, `high`, and `critical` task has explicit human approval before execution
- every `high` and `critical` task receives human review based on evidence, not trust alone
- every waiver is approved by a human with authority to accept the associated risk

## Reviewer expectations

- inspect scope against the spec
- inspect risk controls against the risk assessment
- inspect evidence sufficiency
- record findings and decision in the task record
