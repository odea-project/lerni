# Roles And Responsibilities

## Requester

- states the business need
- provides context and urgency
- confirms acceptance criteria from a business perspective

## Proposer

- creates or refines task records
- drafts scope, assumptions, and deliverables
- ensures metadata completeness before approval routing

## Executor

- performs approved work within scope
- logs meaningful actions and evidence
- stops when assumptions fail or scope changes

## Reviewer

- inspects implementation, evidence, and control compliance
- records findings and requested changes
- decides accept or reject within assigned scope

## Approver

- authorizes execution or acceptance according to delegated authority
- confirms risk tier and required controls
- accepts residual risk where applicable

## Auditor

- independently inspects records and control operation
- documents findings, severity, and remediation status

## Repository maintainer

- manages branch protections, CODEOWNERS, workflow enforcement, and repository settings
- ensures manual hardening guidance is implemented and periodically reviewed
