# Exceptions And Waivers

## Policy

Controls may be waived only through an explicit waiver record. Convenience, deadline pressure, or lack of planning alone are not sufficient justification.

## Waiver requirements

- identify the waived control precisely
- describe why compliance is infeasible or disproportionate
- state the risk being accepted
- define compensating controls
- define an expiry date or review date
- identify the approving human authority
- link the affected task or ADR

## Operational rules

- waivers are time-bounded
- waivers do not silently renew
- expired waivers must block further governed execution until renewed or resolved
- every waiver must be auditable and discoverable from the task record
