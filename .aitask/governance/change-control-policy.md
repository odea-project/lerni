# Change Control Policy

## Scope

This policy governs all changes performed under `.aitask`, including governance changes.

## Requirements

- every change must link to a task ID
- material scope changes require updating `spec.md`, reassessing risk, and refreshing approvals as needed
- governance changes require linked ADR consideration and CODEOWNERS review
- merge readiness depends on task status, evidence completeness, and required approvals

## Out-of-band changes

Emergency or out-of-band changes must be backfilled into `.aitask` records as soon as practical and no later than the next business day, with justification and incident references where applicable.
